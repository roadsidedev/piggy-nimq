# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Piggy serves two audiences equally. Crypto-native savers already holding USDC on Polygon who want yield, borrowing, and social savings features in a polished mobile-first experience. And newcomers to DeFi who need a friendly, guided interface that makes saving and borrowing feel effortless rather than intimidating. Both use Piggy inside Nimiq Pay's Mini App browser — the context is always mobile, always in-wallet.

## Product Purpose

Piggy is an all-in-one savings and borrowing platform that runs inside Nimiq Pay. It helps users deposit USDC into a vault earning Aave V3 yield, borrow against their collateral, set and track savings goals, and participate in social challenges with friends. Success means users save more consistently, earn yield passively, and feel confident managing their money — all without leaving Nimiq Pay.

## Positioning

All-in-one savings vault with Aave yield, borrowing, goals, and challenges — the complete savings platform inside Nimiq Pay.

## Brand Personality

Friendly and playful. Piggy should feel like a financial friend, not a bank — warm, approachable, and gamified. The tone is confident without being serious, helpful without being preachy. Every interaction should feel rewarding, not transactional.

## Anti-references

No specific sites to avoid. The primary guardrail is avoiding AI slop patterns: generic SaaS-cream backgrounds, gradient text, glassmorphism as default, hero-metric templates, identical card grids, and the ubiquitous tiny uppercase eyebrow above every section.

## Design Principles

1. **Effortless by default** — complex DeFi operations (yield, borrowing, health factors) should feel as simple as checking a balance. Hide complexity behind progressive disclosure.
2. **Rewarding every tap** — savings, goal progress, challenges, and yield earnings should feel like wins. Celebrate milestones, show progress, make saving addictive.
3. **Trust through transparency** — financial data demands clarity. Health factors, APY, loan terms — present them honestly without obscuring risk behind friendly UI.
4. **Social by nature** — saving alone is hard. Piggy makes saving communal through challenges, leaderboards, and shared goals. The social layer is a core feature, not an add-on.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Follow best practices: focus trapping in modals, ARIA attributes, reasonable color contrast. No specific WCAG level target — aim for genuine usability across devices and abilities.
